Case Study 01 · Optimisation

Disney+ CTV — Turning Spend Into Subscribers

Disney+ · CTV / PC / Mobile · DV360 · $3M Budget

Campaign Optimisation
The Problem

CTV was consuming 61% of budget but delivering only 34% of conversions. CTR was 18% below benchmark and ROAS sat at 1.4× against a 2.2× target.

What I Did
  • Shifted 22% of CTV budget to PC & mobile (25–34 age bracket converting 2.3× better)
  • A/B tested 2 new creatives, paused fatigued assets
  • Applied 3+ frequency cap on CTV; switched PC bid strategy to target CPA
  • Extended attribution window to 7-day view — resolved CTV conversion lag

Key Takeaway

CTV builds awareness but conversion happens on PC. Rebalancing mid-flight using actual conversion data — not just delivery — was the unlock.
